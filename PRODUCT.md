## Design Context

### Users
Local customers around Fuljhore, Sabji Market, and nearby Durgapur neighborhoods who want to quickly check chicken availability, ask the day's rate, and order by phone or WhatsApp. Most visits should work well on a mobile phone while the customer is at home, at the market, or planning a meal.

### Brand Personality
Clean local shop, direct, and trustworthy. The site should feel like a real neighborhood butcher counter with clear order steps, not a startup landing page or a luxury meat brand.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use the shop logo as the identity anchor: red and warm gold, sturdy typography, practical spacing, and light Bengali touches. Keep WhatsApp green only for WhatsApp and open-status meaning. The page should be mostly light, readable, and fast to scan.

### Design Principles
- Lead with the logo, shop location, phone, WhatsApp, and today's rate flow.
- Do not show stale public prices. Ask customers to confirm the day's rate before payment.
- Use real assets: shop logo, payment QR, and Google Business Profile QR/poster.
- Prefer honest local trust cues over fake ratings, testimonials, or generic quality claims.
- Keep Bengali as a warm accent, not a full bilingual rewrite.
